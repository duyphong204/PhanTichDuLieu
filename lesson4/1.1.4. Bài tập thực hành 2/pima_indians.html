5.2. Phn b cc c trng chnh

In[10]:
diabetes.hist(figsize=(12,10), bins=20, color='#3CB371')
plt.suptitle('Phn b cc c trng trong tp d liu', fontsize=14)
plt.show()

Nhn xt phn b cc c trng trong tp d liu tiu ng

Cc biu histogram cho thy hnh thi phn b ca tng bin. Vic hiu r s phn b l bc quan trng xc nh phng php tin x l d liu ph hp trc khi hun luyn m hnh.

  1. Cc bin c dng phn b chun hoc gn chun Cc bin ny c hnh chung v t b lch, ph hp s dng trc tip hoc sau khi chun ha n gin (Standardization):
  • Glucose (ng huyt): Phn b gn chun, tp trung ch yu quanh mc 100-125.
  • BloodPressure (Huyt p): Phn b gn chun, tp trung mnh quanh mc 70-80.
  • BMI (Ch s khi c th): Phn b gn chun, tp trung ch yu quanh mc 30-35.
  1. Cc bin b lch phi (Right-Skewed) Cc bin ny c ui ko di v pha gi tr ln hn v c nhiu gi tr bng 0 hoc rt nh. Cc bin ny cn c x l (nh bin i logarit) gim lch trc khi a vo m hnh:
  • Pregnancies (S ln mang thai): B lch phi, nhiu ph n c s ln mang thai nh.
  • Insulin: B lch phi rt mnh, a s cc gi tr tp trung mc gn 0, nhng c mt ui di v pha cc gi tr insulin rt cao.
  • SkinThickness ( dy da): B lch phi, vi nhiu gi tr mc thp, c th c gi tr 0 khng hp l (cn kim tra).
  • DiabetesPedigreeFunction: B lch phi, cho thy a s bnh nhn c im chc nng ph h thp.
  • Age (Tui): B lch phi nh, tp trung ch yu tui tr hn (20-30).
  1. Bin mc tiu (Outcome) Outcome (Kt qu) l bin nh phn, cho thy d liu b mt cn bng.
  • 0 (Khng mc bnh): Chim s lng ln hn nhiu (gn 500 mu).
  • 1 (Mc bnh): Chim s lng t hn (khong 250-300 mu). Kt lun: Cn p dng k thut x l d liu mt cn bng (nh SMOTE hoc iu chnh trng s lp) trong qu trnh hun luyn m hnh.

nh gi nhn xt ma trn tng quan

Cc nhn xt km theo biu l chnh xc v hp l.

  1. Mi quan h ch o vi bnh (Outcome)
  • Glucose (0.47): C tng quan dng mnh nht vi Outcome (nguy c mc bnh). ngha: y l kt qu sinh hc quan trng nht ng huyt cng cao th kh nng mc bnh tiu ng cng cao.
  • BMI (0.29), Age (0.24), DiabetesPedigreeFunction (0.17): u c tng quan dng vi Outcome. ngha: Cc yu t v cn nng, tui tc v tin s gia nh u l nhng yu t nguy c quan trng, ng sau ch s ng huyt.
  1. Mi quan h gia cc c trng c lp
  • Pregnancies v Age (0.54): C tng quan dng mnh nht gia cc bin c lp. ngha: S ln mang thai c mi lin h mt thit vi tui tc, cho thy chng c th o lng cng mt kha cnh.
  • SkinThickness v BMI (0.44), SkinThickness v Insulin (0.44): C tng quan dng mnh. ngha: dy da, ch s khi c th v mc insulin c lin h cht ch vi nhau, phn nh mc bo ph v sc khe trao i cht.

6.Kt lun

  • Tp d liu Pima Indians Diabetes gm 768 mu, 8 c trng u vo v 1 bin nhn (Outcome).
  • Khng c d liu thiu, nhng c nhiu gi tr 0 khng hp l cn x l trc khi hun luyn m hnh.
  • Cc c trng c nh hng ln n kt qu bnh gm:
  • Glucose (ng huyt)
  • BMI (ch s khi c th)
  • Age (tui)
  • DiabetesPedigreeFunction (yu t di truyn)

->> D liu sn sng cho bc tin x l v phn loi bng m hnh hc my nhim v tip theo.
