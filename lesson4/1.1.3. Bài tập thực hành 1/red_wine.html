Kt Lun Thng K M T Cht Lng Ru (Summary)

Kt qu thng k m t cho thy s a dng r rt gia cc thnh phn ha hc ca ru vang , vi cht lng tp trung mc trung bnh v s bin ng ln cc cht bo qun.

  1. Cc Yu T Chnh V Cht Lng v Cu Trc
Thuc tnh Trung bnh (mean) std Nhn xt
Quality (Cht lng) $5.64$ $0.81$ Tp trung mc trung bnh. Hu ht cc mu ru u t im $5$ hoc $6$.
Alcohol (Nng cn) $10.42$ $1.07$ Kh n nh. Nng cn t bin ng, cho thy s ng u v mnh.
pH $3.31$ $0.15$ Rt n nh. axit tng th rt ng u gia cc mu.
Density (Mt ) $0.9967$ $0.0019$ Cc k n nh. L thuc tnh t bin ng nht, mt gn nh ng nht.
  1. Cc Yu T Bin ng Cao (Phn Tn Rng)

S khc bit ln nht gia cc mu nm cc cht bo qun v v ngt:

Thuc tnh std Nhn xt v Bin ng
Total Sulfur Dioxide 32.90 Bin ng cc ln. Cho thy s khc bit ng k trong vic x l v hm lng cht bo qun.
Residual Sugar $1.41$ Bin ng cao. nh hng ln n v ngt ca ru.
Free Sulfur Dioxide $10.46$ Bin ng cao. Tng t nh tng SO2, ch ra s a dng trong quy trnh sn xut.

Tm Lc

Tp d liu ny c c im l cht lng tp trung mc trung bnh v s n nh cao v cc thuc tnh cu trc c bn nh pH v density. S bin ng chnh nm sulfur dioxide v residual sugar, gi rng y l nhng yu t then cht cn c phn tch xc

5.Trc quan ha d liu

5.1.Phn b cht lng ru

In[]:
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import seaborn as sns

plt.figure(figsize=(8,5))

sns.countplot(x='quality', data=wine, hue='quality', palette='Set2', legend=False)

plt.title('Phn b cht lng ru ')
plt.xlabel('im cht lng')
plt.ylabel('S lng mu')
plt.show()

Nhn xt:

  • Phn ln ru c im 56, s mu c im cao (78) t hn nhiu.
  • y l tp d liu mt cn bng nh.

5.2. Mi quan h gia nng cn v cht lng

In[]:
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import seaborn as sns

plt.figure(figsize=(8,5))

sns.boxplot(x='quality', y='alcohol', data=wine, hue='quality', palette='coolwarm', legend=False)

plt.title('Mi quan h gia Alcohol v Quality')
plt.xlabel('im cht lng') # Thm nhn trc X cho r rng
plt.ylabel('Nng  Cn (Alcohol)') # Thm nhn trc Y cho r rng
plt.show()

Nhn xt:

  • Ru c nng cn cao thng c nh gi cht lng tt hn.
  • C xu hng tng dn r rng t quality = 3 n quality = 8.

6.Kt lun

  • B d liu Red Wine Quality gm 1599 mu, 12 c trng v 1 nhn cht lng.
  • Khng c d liu thiu, ch c vi dng trng lp.
  • Cc c trng lin quan cht ch n cht lng gm:
  • alcohol (cn) tc ng tch cc
  • volatile acidity ( axit d bay hi) tc ng tiu cc
  • sulphates, citric acid c nh hng nh

=> D liu sn sng cho bc tin x l v hun luyn m hnh phn loi nhim v tip theo.
